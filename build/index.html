The workflow

Record your browser

Testless will record all your interactions with the Chrome or Chromium. It will suggest intelligently checks to be performed. It offers custom checks and actions for you as well.

Replay your session

You can instantly replay your session and see if Testless can reproduce your reseults. You can store your tests on your disk only, export them to Google Drive, Dropbox, Github, Bitbucket, Gitlab or store them with us. There format is human readable and may serve as a documentation of all features.

Replay automatically with every release

Replay these test on a regularly schedulded basis or whenever the page changes, eg. after a new release. Receive E-Mail, SMS or Slack notification if something breaks. Then you can analyze whether these changes a tolerable and just accept them. You can export these changes in form of release notes.

Integration & third party support

It performs automatically industry proven audit of Google Lighthouse, valid W3C and JSON-LD. It ships as a docker image and integrates with all modern CI/CD pipelines. You can find examples for integrating with CircleCI, AppVeyor, Jenkins, TravisCI, Bamboo. Testless integrates with Selenium and services like Sauce Labs, Browserstack or TestingBot. If you need a self hosted version of the whole platform, we provide that too.
